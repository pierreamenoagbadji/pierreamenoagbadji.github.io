Since February 2026, I am a postdoc in the AN-EDP team in the Mathematics Department of Orsay. My advisor is Antoine Levitt.

From January 2024 to February 2026, I was a postdoc in the Applied Physics and Applied Mathematics (APAM) department of Columbia University, under the mentorship of Michael Weinstein.

From October 2020 to December 2023, I was a PhD student in the POEMS team at Ensta Paris, under the supervision of Sonia Fliss and Patrick Joly.

My research focuses on the theoretical study and the numerical analysis of wave propagation phenomena in heterogeneous (periodic or quasiperiodic) structures.
