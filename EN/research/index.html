Research interests

My current research interests lie in the mathematical study and the numerical simulation of time-harmonic wave propagation phenomena in heterogeneous ― periodic or quasiperiodic ― structures. Because the corresponding problems are posed in infinite domains, I am interested in:

  • designing numerical methods to truncate the computational domain, without inducing any spurious reflexions;
  • the study of the limiting absorption principle;
  • in the context of my postdoc, the study and the numerical computation of guided modes along lineic defects.
